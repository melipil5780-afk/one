Why EMDR?

When trauma occurs, it gets "locked" in the nervous system. Standard talk therapy addresses the mind, but EMDR addresses the brain's processing centers to unlock lasting relief.

01. Assessment

We identify the core target memories that are currently driving your triggers and anxiety.

02. Desensitization

Using bilateral stimulation to neutralize the emotional charge of traumatic events.

03. Integration

Installing positive beliefs and a renewed sense of safety within your body and mind.
